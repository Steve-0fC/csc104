Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50g Parmesan Cheese
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk the eggs and Parmesan together in a bowl.
  4. Combine the cooked spaghetti with pancetta and egg mixture. Toss and season with pepper.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

Chicken Tacos

Ingredients

  • 500g Chicken Breast
  • 8 Small Tortillas
  • Fresh Salsa
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Avocado

Instructions

  1. Grill the chicken and cut it into strips.
  2. Warm the tortillas and assemble tacos with chicken, salsa, lettuce, cheese, and avocado.
  3. Serve with a side of lime wedges!

Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Broccoli
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 Carrot
  • 200g Tofu
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Sesame Seeds

Instructions

  1. Chop vegetables and tofu into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Stir-fry the vegetables and tofu in olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add soy sauce and sesame seeds, toss to coat.
  4. Serve with rice and enjoy!
